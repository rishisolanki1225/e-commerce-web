How soon can I get my orders?

We make sure our customers receive their orders in 3-5 business days. You'll receive an email containing the order details, the tracking number for the parcel, and the official receipt as soon as your items are handed over for delivery.

How safe are the payment options?

We protect your financial information through the necessary security measures: partnerships with trusted payment processors, fraud detection tools, and two-factor authentication.

How often do you restock?

To ensure you don't miss out on your favorites, we make sure to restock monthly. However, this also depends on the product availability.
